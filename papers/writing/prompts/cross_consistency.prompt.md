name: cross_consistency
description: Check consistency across sections
---

Audit cross-paper consistency.

Check:
- title vs abstract
- abstract vs results
- introduction claims vs evidence
- methods vs reported experiments
- results vs conclusion
- figure/table wording vs body text
- terminology consistency
- model naming consistency
- metric naming consistency
- claimed novelty vs actual content

Output:
--- CROSS-CONSISTENCY REPORT ---
List inconsistencies with severity and suggested fix.
Do not rewrite globally.
